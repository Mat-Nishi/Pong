Controls:
    Menu: Up and Down Arrow to navigate, 'x' to confirm

    Player One: w -> Up
                s -> Down
                a -> Left  (only on free movement mode)
                d -> Right (only on free movement mode)

    Player One: Up Arrow -> up
                Down Arrow -> down
                Left Arrow -> left  (only on free movement mode)
                Right Arrow -> right (only on free movement mode)
